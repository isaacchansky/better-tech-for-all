What if efforts to improve people's lives had no technical barriers?

Too many of the organizations that work every day to make people's lives better and to help us all in our daily lives struggle to achieve a strong level of techincal support. Whether that's lacking a knowledgeable user experience researcher, a strong design lead, or a proficient developer. It's our hope that by assembling those willing to help, we can alleviate some of the barriers for these organizations hoping to do good in the world, and eventually raise the baseline standard of technology and design in these areas.
